What is ONYX MUN?

ONYX MUN 2026 is Hyderabad's premier Model United Nations conference — and the event most delegates search for when they ask for the best MUN in Hyderabad. From 12 to 14 June 2026, students from schools and colleges across India convene at Laurus The Universal School, Bowrampet, for three days of structured diplomacy, debate, and leadership development under the tagline that defines the edition: serious committees, serious debate, zero compromise on academic quality.

ONYX runs seven specialised committees, each with a published agenda and an experienced Executive Board: UNGA-DISEC (anti-satellite weapons and dual-use space technology), Continuous Crisis Committee (CCC), UNHRC (human rights of seafarers), UNCSW (access to justice for women), Lok Sabha (border security and trade), International Press, and Illuminati. Delegates of all experience levels are welcome — from first-time participants building confidence in their first committee room to seasoned chairs who expect protocol-led, research-driven sessions.

Why ONYX is Hyderabad's premier MUN

Hyderabad's Model UN circuit has grown rapidly, but ONYX MUN 2026 stands apart for how it combines academic rigour with a premium on-ground experience.
