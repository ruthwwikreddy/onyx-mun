Delegate Policy

1. Code of Conduct

ONYX MUN 2026 is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and professional environment for all participants. Every delegate, EB member, and secretariat staff is expected to uphold the highest standards of diplomacy.

  • Professionalism: Delegates must maintain a diplomatic decorum in and out of the council rooms.
  • Anti-Harassment: Any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying based on gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, or disability is strictly prohibited.
  • Zero Tolerance: Substance abuse, physical violence, or possession of prohibited items will lead to immediate expulsion from the conference.

2. Dress Code

To be informed by the Secretariat.

3. Academic Integrity

Delegates are evaluated on their original research and negotiation skills. Plagiarism is a serious offense.

  • Position papers and speeches must be the delegate's own work.
  • AI tools may be used for research assistance, but verbatim AI-generated speeches or papers will be flagged.
  • Honesty in negotiation and adherence to the council’s rules of procedure is required.

4. Post-Allocation Requirement

Once committee and country allocations are released, all delegates are required to formally accept their position.

  • Delegates must sign an 'Agreement of Participation' form within 48 hours of receiving their allocation.
  • Additional details (travel info, ID proof) must be submitted via the secondary form provided in the allocation email.
  • Failure to comply with these requirements may lead to the forfeiture of your allocated seat.

5. Attendance & Certification

  • Attendance at every session is mandatory to receive a certificate of participation.
  • Delegates arriving late or leaving early without prior permission from the chair will have their participation status reviewed.

5. Liability & Waiver

The ONYX MUN Secretariat and the host institution are not liable for any loss of personal property or injuries sustained during the conference. By registering, delegates agree to follow all safety guidelines provided by the staff.
