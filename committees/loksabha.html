Border Security & Trade

Debating the balance between securing India's borders and facilitating trade.

The Committee

The Lok Sabha simulates India's lower house of Parliament. Delegates represent constituencies and parties while legislating on security and commerce.

Key debate questions

  • Can border security be strengthened without slowing legitimate trade?
  • How should Parliament fund surveillance vs. border-state development?
  • What role should private industry play in customs modernisation?

Discussion framework

  • Border infrastructure and surveillance modernisation.
  • Trade corridors and customs efficiency.
  • Regional development in border states.

Council brief

Indian parliamentary procedure — rhetoric and whip discipline matter.
