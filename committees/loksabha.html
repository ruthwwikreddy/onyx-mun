The House

The Lok Sabha represents the pulse of India's democracy. As the lower house of the bicameral Parliament, it holds the power to shape national legislation, oversee executive functions, and reflect the diverse voices of over a billion citizens.

From economic reforms to social justice, the Lok Sabha at ONYX MUN provides a platform for delegates to simulate parliamentary debate with accuracy and gravity.

Parliamentary Scope

  • Economic policy and the path to global leadership.
  • Social welfare reforms and inclusive national development.
  • National security protocols and strategic defense policy.
  • Environmental legislation and sustainable urban growth.
  • Educational and healthcare reforms for a modern India.

Expectations

Delegates are expected to master parliamentary procedures, represent their party's stance with conviction, and work towards meaningful national legislation.
