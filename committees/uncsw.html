Commission on the Status of Women

UNCSW

Gender equality through institutional reform.

Strengthening Access to Justice

Advancing women's and girls' access to justice systems — removing legal, social, and economic barriers.

The Committee

UNCSW leads global norm-setting on gender equality. Delegates will craft resolutions that strengthen judicial access and survivor-centred institutions.

Discussion framework

  • Legal aid and representation for women and marginalised groups.
  • Reform of discriminatory laws and practices.
  • Intersectionality in policy design.

Council brief

A committee for delegates focused on feminist policy and institutional change.
