Protecting dignity and labour rights at sea.

Human Rights of Seafarers

Examining the protection of seafarers' human rights, including fair labour conditions, safety at sea, and access to justice for maritime workers.

The Committee

The UN Human Rights Council addresses systemic violations and promotes accountability. This session centres on seafarers who sustain global trade.

Discussion framework

  • Working conditions, rest hours, and mental health at sea.
  • Flag-state and port-state responsibility.
  • Access to remedy for abuse and abandonment.

Council brief

Ideal for delegates passionate about humanitarian law and labour rights.
